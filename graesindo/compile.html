PT. Graha Estetika Indonesia
Graha Estetika Indonesia is a Design and Built based construction company (General Contractor) which was founded in 2013. Our company serves various projects such as architecture, interior, landscape, civil and Mechanical-Electrical in residential buildings, offices and buildings. The project serves the Jabodetabek area and surrounding areas.
Graha Estetika Indonesia merupakan perusahaan konstruksi (General Contractor) yang berbasis Design and Built yang didirikan pada tahun 2013. Perusahaan kami melayani berbagai projek seperti arsitektur, interior, landscape, sipil dan ME pada bangunan rumah tinggal, kantor dan gedung. Projek kami melayani area Jabodetabek dan sekitarnya.

Visi

Misi
